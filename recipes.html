Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches are a simple and quick meal.

What you'll need:

  • Two slices of bread
  • A spoonful of peanut butter
  • A spoonful of jelly

How to assemble:

  1. Take your two slices of bread and lay them flat on a plate.
  2. Spread the spoonful of peanut butter one the inside of one of the slices of bread.
  3. Spread the spoonful of jelly on the inside of the other slice of bread.
  4. Combine the two slices of bread, connecting the peanut butter to the jelly.
  5. Eat and enjoy!

Instant Ramen Noodles

Nothing is easier and more delicious than instant ramen, especially the soy sauce flavor of Top Ramen.

What you'll need:

  • A small saucepan or pot
  • Two cups of water
  • One pack of Top Ramen Soy Sauce Flavor

How to assemble:

  1. Add two cups of water to a small saucepan or pot.
  2. Place your saucepan or pot on the stovetop and bring to a boil.
  3. Add your block of noodles from the pack to the pot. Place the flavor packet to the side. Boil the noodles for 2 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and add the soy sauce flavor packet to the pot. Stir well.
  5. Optional: pour ramen into a bowl. Otherwise, just eat it straight from the pot!

The Best Vegan Sandwich

This is my go-to lunch when I'm feeling inspired. Super simple and delicious!

What you'll need:

  • Spray oil
  • Two slices of bread of your choice
  • Two spoonfuls of Vegenaise
  • A slice of meltable vegan cheese
  • A slice of Tofurky Plant-Based Deli Slices
  • Two slices of tomato
  • 3-4 dill pickle chips
  • 3-4 slices of banana peppers
  • A piece of lettuce
  • A sprinkle of garlic salt and pepper
  • A panini press

How to assemble:

  1. Turn on your panini press. Once it is hot enough, spray your oil on both sides.
  2. While your panini press is heating up, place your two slices of bread on a plate. Add your spoonfuls of Vegenaise to the inside of each slice.
  3. Add your slice of vegan cheese to the inside of the bottom slice of bread.
  4. Add your Tofurky slice on top of the vegan cheese.
  5. Add your slice of tomato on top of your Tofurky. Then, add your pickles and banana peppers.
  6. Top off your sandwich ingredients with the piece of lettuce.
  7. Sprinkle your garlic salt and pepper on top of both sides of the sandwich.
  8. Close the sandwich. Add the sandwich to your panini press.
  9. Check on the sandwich occasionally. Remove it from the panini press when you are satisfied with how cooked it is. Turn off the panini press.
  10. Let the sandwich cool for a bit. Eat and enjoy!
