Travel

Chicago, Illinois

Chicago is a fun place to visit every so often, however, I would not want to live there.

My hometown is about 30 miles northwest of Chicago, so I have been there quite a few times. With as many fun experiences I have had in the city, I have also had my fair share of not-so-fun experiences. Most of them involve using the Chicago public transportation system.

Photo Gallery

Pros of Visiting

  • Can see The Bean
  • Good selection of vegan food
  • Lollapalooza is fun

Cons of Visiting

  • CTA system is scary and confusing
  • Can get really cold
  • Not that interesting

Austin, Texas

Austin, Texas is probably my favorite place I have visited. Their selection of vegan food is phenomenal. It's not cold like the midwest. I would love to live there one day.

Photo Gallery

Pros of Visiting

  • Even better vegan food
  • Not super cold, and the heat is dry instead of humid
  • Live Music Capital of the World

Cons of Visiting

  • Not super walkable
  • Can get very hot in the summer
  • It's in Texas

Ann Arbor, Michigan

Ann Arbor, Michigan is a beautiful city. I had never considered applying to the University of Michigan until I visited my friend that went here when I was a sophomore at my community college in Illinois. I instantly fell in love with the city and was thrilled when I was accepted into the school. I love this town and will be sad to leave it one day.

Photo Gallery

Pros of Visiting

  • Lots of awesome vegan food
  • Super walkable and safe at night
  • A lot of stuff to do for college students

Cons of Visiting

  • It's a college town, so it's not for everyone
  • It gets pretty cold
  • No one knows how to drive here
